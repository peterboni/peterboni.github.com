PhotoArchive for iPhone

February 22, 2014

PhotoArchive saves your irreplaceable memories, your photos & videos from your iOS device Camera Roll to your FTP server over Wi-Fi.

Most NAS (Network Attached Storage) devices support FTP (File Transfer Protocol), so you can PhotoArchive directly to your NAS. FTP should only be used on Wi-Fi with WPA2 security. Be security smart.

  1. Save Money – When you use PhotoArchive you can turn off the Camera Roll from iCloud Backup, and stay under the 5.0 GB free limit. Use iCloud Backup to back up your accounts, documents, and settings.

  2. Save Time – Stop manually backing up your photos & videos to your Mac (are you still using USB cables?). Stop manually backing up your iOS device to iTunes!

  3. Archives – PhotoArchive does not synchronise. PhotoArchive will not download photos or videos from your FTP server to your iOS device.

  4. Never Deletes – PhotoArchive will never delete a photo or video from your FTP server, or from your iOS device.

  5. Smart Archive – PhotoArchive remembers the previous archive, and will only upload new photos & videos, so it’s FAST!

  6. Smart Photo View – Shows you the photos & videos you have not archived (in danger of being lost forever), and the ones you have (safe in the event that something happens to your iOS device).

  7. Full Resolution & Metadata – PhotoArchive uploads photos & videos in their full resolution with all metadata.

Download PhotoArchive on the App Store (… coming soon!)

Requires iOS 7.0 or later.
Compatible with iPhone, iPad and iPod touch.
This app is optimised for iPhone 5.
